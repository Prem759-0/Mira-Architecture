About Studio

We craft spaces that
hold memory.

MIRA Studio was founded on the belief that architecture is not merely the art of building — it is the art of creating places where human life unfolds. Every project begins with listening.

Our work spans private residences, cultural institutions, and urban interventions across Europe and beyond. We are a studio of twelve, deliberate in scale, ambitious in vision.

02 — How we work

A deliberate process.

01
Discovery
We begin every project with deep listening — understanding site, client, culture, and climate before a single line is drawn.
02
Concept
Sketches, models, and material studies converge into a singular idea that drives all decisions from structure to detail.
03
Design
The concept is developed into precise technical documentation, working with our network of engineers and craftspeople.
04
Realisation
We remain closely involved through construction, ensuring the built work matches the vision — and often surpasses it.
